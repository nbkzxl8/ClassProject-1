No Attorney's Fees Unless We Win

At the Law Office of Michael R. Roby, we will not charge you unless we win your case. We dedicate our firm to exclusively fighting for you when a person, corporation or insurance company acts unsafe and injures you. We try and settle cases to get great recoveries for our clients. So, our personal injury lawyers work on a contingency-fee basis. You will not owe any fees or expenses unless we win your case. Our firm is well financed and we have the resources and expertise to win your personal injury case.

If you suffered injuries as the result of an accident or an intentional act, you may have the right to collect compensation. In worst-case scenarios, a person may face permanent disabilities or even death. Our firm can help you establish personal injury liability in an attempt to hold the responsible parties legally liable for their actions in civil court. A qualified attorney aims to obtain full compensation for not just medical bills, but also lost time at work, diminished quality of life, and mental anguish. An accident injury attorney can help provide you with the peace of mind necessary for your recovery.
